Club Recognized as Nonprofit Society

On April 19, the Oceanside Chess Club reached a significant milestone by becoming an officially incorporated nonprofit society. This achievement comes after submitting the application earlier this month, marking a new chapter in the club's journey. Incorporation not only formalizes our structure but also strengthens our ability to serve the chess community and pursue our mission of promoting the game.

As the club continues to grow, this step reflects our commitment to building a sustainable and thriving organization.
