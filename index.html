Oceanside Chess

A nonprofit society empowering communities through the game of chess across the Mid-Island.

Meet Your Community

At Oceanside Chess, we're a community of players passionate about spreading our love of chess across Vancouver Island, and we invite you to join us!

Join Contact

Who Is Oceanside Chess For?

Oceanside Chess welcomes players of all skill levels, from complete beginners to seasoned masters. Our community is built for anyone with an interest in the game.

Beginners

New to chess? Learn the fundamentals, basic strategies, and develop your skills in a supportive environment.

Intermediate Players

Already know the basics? Improve your tactical awareness, opening repertoire, and positional understanding.

Advanced Players

Refine your endgame technique, study complex positions, and compete in challenging tournaments.
