Oceanside Chess

Welcome to Oceanside Chess! We are a non-profit society based in Qualicum Beach dedicated to bringing chess to the mid-island.

Our Mission

Oceanside Chess is dedicated to providing a welcoming environment to learn, study, and enjoy the game of chess. Stop by on Saturdays from 11am to 3pm at the St. Mark's Church for in-person games and tons of fun!
